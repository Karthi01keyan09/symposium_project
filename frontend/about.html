About The Symposium

The Tech Symposium is a national level technical event organized to bring together innovative minds, creative thinkers, and technology enthusiasts from different colleges. The event provides a platform for students to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and compete in various technical and non-technical events.

Participants will engage in coding competitions, debugging challenges, quizzes, treasure hunts, and other exciting activities designed to test their knowledge, teamwork, and creativity.
